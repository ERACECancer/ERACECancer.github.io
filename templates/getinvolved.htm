Volunteer at PFC

The Pacific Free Clinic (PFC) is a student-run clinic that offers health screenings, basic medications, and referrals to patients in and around San Jose. PFC recruits bilingual undergraduates who serve as both interpreters and patient advocates. If you are considering a career in healthcare, working with the PFC team can be a valuable introduction to working with underserved patient population.

Volunteer at Arbor

The Arbor Free Clinic is open every Sunday from 8:30 AM to 2 PM and offers culturally appropriate high quality transitional medical care for patients in communities around Stanford. Located within the Menlo Park VA, the Arbor clinic is an easy commute from the Stanford campus and a great opportunity to work with a team of Stanford undergrads, medical students, and MDs to help address health disparities.
