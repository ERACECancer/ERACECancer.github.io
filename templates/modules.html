In speaking with community member about cancer prevention and early detection, we found multiple misconceptions around screening recommendations: What cancers had screening tests? How frequently do experts recommend being screened? What factors increase the importance of screening? Our Cancer Screening Flowchart is an attempt to answer these questions.
