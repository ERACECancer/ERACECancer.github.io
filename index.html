About Us

ERACE Cancer is a group of students and researchers working to Establish Race And Class Equality in cancer. We lead educational and outreach initiatives focused on cancer prevention in at-risk populations in the Bay Area.

In addition, we serve as a forum for discussions on the social determinants of health and the societal fac- tors that shape access to adequate healthcare.
